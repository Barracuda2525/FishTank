About Karal Ankuda

I was born January 7th in Boston Massachusetts. I moved to New Jersey before I turned one, living in a small town called Maple Shade.

I attended the local school, Maple Shade High School. I played four years of varsity football and currently hold the record for most career tackles at the school.

I also played baseball my junior and senior year.

I graduated high school in 2013.

I joined the United States Army in May of 2015 and graduated Basic Training at Fort Benning in July.

I spent four years active duty as a Military Intelligence Systems Maintainer/Integrator at Fort Campbell, Kentucky.

In March of 2019, I finished my active duty contract and moved back to New Jersey.

I am currently in the Army Reserves while attending college at Rowan University.

I am on pace to graduate as a C&I Major this upcoming spring then hope to find a good IT job in the South Jersey/Philly region.
